Our Story

GethisGreen is an American brand inspired in Africa that reflects my vision; to bring inspiration and innovation to every Go-Getter! The brand resonates ambition, passion and consistency to serve its clients by offering an esteemed product specially designed with you in mind!

GethisGreen was founded in 2020 by a young Nigerian college student motivated by the extreme hardship in West Africa to fulfill his childhood dream of pioneering an exclusive world-leading brand offering “functional” luxury products to its clients.

In supporting GethisGreen, our distinguished clients rank among the many who voluntarily contribute towards improving the overall lifestyle of poverty-stricken children in West Africa.

On a final and highly important note, we look forward to you enjoying our products as much as we do; and never give up ;)
